# DooR DasH: Scare vs Laugh Touchdown 🚪👹

A turn-based, 2-player board game simulator set in the *Monsters, Inc.* universe, built for **CSEN401**.

## 🎮 How to Play
* **Objective:** Be the first player to land **exactly on Cell 99** with **>= 1,000 energy units**. 
***Looping:** If you pass Cell 99 without enough energy, your position wraps back to the beginning of the board.
* **Movement:** Roll a standard 6-sided die to advance along the 100-cell zigzag grid.
* **Collision:** You cannot land on a cell occupied by the opponent. If blocked, your turn is invalid and you must re-roll.

## 🗺️ Board Mechanics
* **Door Cells (50):** Land on your team's door to gain energy for the whole team; land on an opponent's door to lose energy. Doors exhaust after one use.
* **Transport Cells (10):** Conveyor Belts launch you forward.Contamination Socks drag you backward and drain 100 energy.
***Monster Cells (6):** Trigger free character powerups for teammates, or swap energy levels with an opponent.
* **Card Cells (10):** Draw a random card from a shuffled 25-card deck (includes Swappers, Steals, Shields, and Role Confusion).

## ⚡ Characters Archetypes
* **Dynamo (Sulley/Yeti):** Energy gains and losses are completely doubled[.
* **Dasher (Mike/Fungus):** Moves at 2x base speed.
* **Multitasker (Celia/Roz):** Moves at half speed, but gets a +200 bonus to all energy changes.
* **Schemer (Randall/Waternoose):** Gets a +10 bonus to all energy changes.
