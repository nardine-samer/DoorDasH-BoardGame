# DooR DasH: Scare vs Laugh Touchdown 🚪👹

A turn-based, 2-player board game simulator set in the *Monsters, Inc.* universe, built for **CSEN401**[cite: 1, 3, 20].

## 🎮 How to Play
* **Objective:** Be the first player to land **exactly on Cell 99** with **>= 1,000 energy units**[cite: 16, 332, 333, 334]. 
***Looping:** If you pass Cell 99 without enough energy, your position wraps back to the beginning of the board[cite: 128, 335].
* **Movement:** Roll a standard 6-sided die to advance along the 100-cell zigzag grid[cite: 14, 273, 310, 311].
* **Collision:** You cannot land on a cell occupied by the opponent. [cite_start]If blocked, your turn is invalid and you must re-roll[cite: 314, 315, 316].

## 🗺️ Board Mechanics
* **Door Cells (50):** Land on your team's door to gain energy for the whole team; land on an opponent's door to lose energy[cite: 135, 136, 137]. [cite_start]Doors exhaust after one use[cite: 138].
* **Transport Cells (10):** Conveyor Belts launch you forward[cite: 153, 156]. [cite_start]Contamination Socks drag you backward and drain 100 energy[cite: 160].
***Monster Cells (6):** Trigger free character powerups for teammates, or swap energy levels with an opponent[cite: 145, 146, 194].
* **Card Cells (10):** Draw a random card from a shuffled 25-card deck (includes Swappers, Steals, Shields, and Role Confusion)[cite: 130, 166, 189].

## ⚡ Characters Archetypes
* **Dynamo (Sulley/Yeti):** Energy gains and losses are completely doubled[cite: 204, 206, 207].
* **Dasher (Mike/Fungus):** Moves at 2x base speed[cite: 196, 200].
* **Multitasker (Celia/Roz):** Moves at half speed, but gets a +200 bonus to all energy changes[cite: 211, 214, 215].
* **Schemer (Randall/Waternoose):** Gets a +10 bonus to all energy changes[cite: 219, 222].
